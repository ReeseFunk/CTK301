Thunderstorms are weather events that can be both fascinating and frightening. They are characterized by the presence of thunder and lightning, and are often accompanied by heavy rainfall and strong winds. While thunderstorms can occur at any time of the year, they are most common in spring and summer, when the atmosphere is warm and moist. Thunderstorms are formed when warm, moist air rises and meets cooler air in the atmosphere, causing the moisture in the air to condense and form clouds. These clouds can grow to become cumulonimbus clouds, which are tall, dark and ominous-looking.

The game is part of the Legend of Zelda series and is known for its unique gameplay mechanics and dark, eerie atmosphere.
